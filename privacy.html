Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Who we are

Secumind is operated by Mikkel Nielsen. For privacy matters, contact mikkar@proton.me.

What data we collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the service:

  • Contact form submissions — name, email address, and message content you voluntarily submit.
  • Infrastructure data you connect — domains, IP ranges, and log sources you configure inside the platform. This data is processed solely to run the security analysis you request and is never sold or shared.
  • Usage data — basic analytics (page views, referrer) to understand how the site is used. No cross-site tracking.

How we use your data

  • To respond to demo requests and support enquiries.
  • To run the security scans and generate reports you have configured.
  • To improve the product based on aggregate usage patterns.

Data storage and transfer

Infrastructure scan data is processed on your own infrastructure when you use the self-hosted deployment option. For cloud-hosted tenants, data is stored in the EU. We do not transfer personal data outside the EEA without appropriate safeguards.

Your rights (GDPR)

If you are based in the EU or EEA, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Object to or restrict processing.
  • Lodge a complaint with your national data protection authority.

To exercise any of these rights, email mikkar@proton.me.

Cookies

We use a single first-party cookie to remember your light/dark theme preference. No advertising or tracking cookies are set.

Third-party services

AI analysis features are powered by Anthropic Claude. When AI analysis is enabled, finding data is sent to Anthropic's API for processing. This is configurable and can be disabled. See Anthropic's privacy policy for details.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the product evolves. Material changes will be communicated by email to active customers.
