FAQ

Questions, answered.

If yours isn't here, hit contact and you'll get a direct reply.

Is this a replacement for a traditional pentest?

No — and we don't pretend it is. Secumind sits between your annual pentests, so the gap between formal engagements stops being a blind spot. Many of our customers run both.

Where does my data go?

Detection logic runs entirely on your infrastructure. The only outbound call is the optional AI analysis (to Anthropic Claude). That call is configurable and can be disabled for fully air-gapped deployments.

What about false positives?

Every finding has an "accepted risk" status you can apply with a note. Once marked, the same fingerprint won't surface as new on subsequent scans.

Do you integrate with my existing tools?

Discord and Slack webhooks ship today. Ticket-create webhooks for Linear / Jira / GitHub Issues are configurable. SSO (Google, Microsoft) on the Company tier.

How long does setup take?

About twenty minutes for the first target. Most teams have their first round of findings within an hour.

Is the audit report really auditor-grade?

Reports are HMAC-SHA256 signed, with verifiable integrity — auditors can confirm nothing was edited after generation. Findings map to MITRE ATT&CK and CVE identifiers, which is the language SOC 2 and ISO 27001 reviewers already use.

Can I self-host?

Yes, on the Company tier. Self-hosted deployments are fully air-gapped if AI analysis is disabled, and you can wire the dashboard to your own SSO and Postgres instance.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes — the Solo tier comes with a 14-day trial. The Team and Company tiers start with a guided walkthrough so we can match the configuration to your environment.

What stack does Secumind run on?

Python + Flask backend with Postgres for auth and SQLite for the findings inbox. Anthropic Claude for AI analysis. Runs on any Linux server, or as a Docker container.

How do I get my findings out of Secumind?

Every finding is exportable as signed JSON. Reports come as JSON, HTML, and (soon) PDF. The data is yours — no vendor lock-in.
