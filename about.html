About

Affordable security operations.
For teams without a SOC.

Secumind exists because most companies sit between two bad options: pay for a pentest they can only afford once a year, or fly blind for the eleven months in between.

Why we're building this

Most small and mid-market teams can't justify €20–60k for a red team engagement they get one snapshot from. But they're still asked the same security questionnaire questions as the teams that can.

Secumind closes that gap. Continuous attack simulations, internal threat visibility, and signed audit evidence — without the SOC headcount or the consultancy invoice.

We're not trying to replace your annual pentest. We're trying to make sure the eleven months between them aren't a blind spot.

Principles

How we build.

Capability over fear

We don't sell with breach statistics. We sell with what the product actually does. If a feature can't be demoed in 60 seconds, it doesn't ship.

Your data stays yours

Detection logic runs on your infrastructure. The only outbound call is the optional AI analysis — configurable, auditable, and removable.

Outcomes, not features

Three pillars. Three outcomes. No SKU menu. You shouldn't need an enablement manager to understand what you're paying for.

Public roadmap

What we're working on now, next, and later — published. No dark launches. If you bought the product, you know what's coming.
