Product

One platform.
Three outcomes.

Every feature in Secumind serves one of three outcomes — Continuous Threat Exposure, Internal Threat Visibility, and AI Security Operations. No sprawl. No fragmented tools.

Pillar 01 · Continuous Threat Exposure

What an attacker would find — every day, not once a year.

Secumind runs AI-driven attack simulations against your infrastructure, correlates every discovered service to live CVE intelligence, and tracks how your external attack surface changes between scans.

A traditional pentest is a snapshot in time. Secumind reruns on a schedule — so the report you hand an auditor reflects last week, not last quarter.

Capabilities

  • AI-driven attack simulations mapped to MITRE ATT&CK
  • Continuous CVE correlation against the NVD
  • External surface monitoring: SSL expiry, DNS changes, exposed ports, breach exposure
  • Drift detection that flags new hosts, new services, and new vulnerabilities
  • nmap-backed network reconnaissance
  • Plugin-based attack chain extensions

Capabilities

  • Live nginx + SSH event ingestion
  • Automatic brute-force and credential-stuffing detection
  • Failed-then-successful login alerting
  • Lateral movement and privilege escalation signals
  • Dark web credential exposure monitoring
  • Active TCP connection visibility
Pillar 02 · Internal Threat Visibility

What's already moving inside — before someone notices on the news.

Most breaches go undetected for weeks because no one's watching the auth logs, the web logs, or the dark web. Secumind watches all three, continuously, and surfaces the patterns that matter.

Catches account-takeover attempts in progress, not after the customer email goes out.

Pillar 03 · AI Security Operations

A senior analyst's report, generated on a schedule.

Every finding from every pillar feeds a single AI-driven workflow that ranks risk, drafts remediations, and produces signed, tamper-proof audit reports ready for SOC 2, ISO 27001, and cyber-insurance evidence packs.

Built on top of Anthropic Claude. Outputs are signed with HMAC-SHA256, so auditors can verify nothing was changed after generation.

Capabilities

  • AI-driven risk assessment and attack narrative
  • HMAC-SHA256 signed, verifiable reports
  • Scheduled scans with Discord, Slack, webhook alerts
  • Plain-English security awareness briefings
  • Audit evidence packs (SOC 2, ISO 27001)
  • Weekly AI executive summary (roadmap)
