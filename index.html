Built around three outcomes, not nine tools.

Every feature in Secumind serves one of three outcomes. Memorable, parallel, easy to operate.

01

Continuous Threat Exposure

What an attacker would find — every day, not once a year.

AI-driven attack simulations against your infrastructure, with every discovered service correlated to live CVE intelligence.

  • MITRE ATT&CK-mapped attack simulations
  • Continuous CVE correlation from the NVD
  • SSL, DNS, exposed port monitoring

Internal Threat Visibility

What's already moving inside — before someone notices on the news.

Live monitoring of auth events, web traffic, and dark web exposure — surfacing the patterns that signal compromise in progress.

  • Automatic brute-force and credential-stuffing detection

  • Dark web credential exposure monitoring
